How to Chain Judgement Cut

Judgement Cut is one of player Vergil's starting abilities. It can follow any attack or taunt involving Yamato, can be performed both on the ground and in mid-air, and has a long range.

It can be executed with perfect timing, for this the button must be released just as the scabbard starts sparkling after an attack, dash, or the sword sheathing. Perfect Cut execution window is slightly longer in the air.

Perfect Judgement Cuts can be performed in quick succession up to 3 times, with the 3rd perfect Cut being indicated by Vergil's "Jackpot". To do it, To do it, perform a Judgement Cut by holding and releasing the attack button. AS soon as you release it, immediately holdit down for a bit and release it again, rinse and repeat. Under normal circumstances the maximum number of consecutive Judgement Cuts is 4 (a normal one followed by 3 perfect Cuts), however, by cancelling the string with Enemy Step or Trick Action before the 3rd Cut is out, it is possible to keep performing consecutive Cuts indefinitely, while staying in the air.
